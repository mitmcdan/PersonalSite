College classes are built to build foundations and show you what to expect, they do not offer any sort of real expierence. I am very greatful to my dad and the men at Ash who let me work here despite my infancy in the world of development. I feel like I might be over-qualified for the job but it will be a good opportunity to help others and who knows, I might just learn a thing or two.
